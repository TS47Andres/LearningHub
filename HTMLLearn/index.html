About HTMLLearn

HTMLearn is a focused online platform designed to guide you to proficiency in HTML in just 8 weeks. Our curriculum is structured to provide a clear and engaging learning experience, covering the fundamental concepts and practical skills needed to structure content for the web.

Whether you're starting your web development journey or looking to solidify your understanding of semantic HTML, HTMLLearn provides the resources you need.

Our 8-Week HTML Syllabus

What HTML is, setup, basic tags.

  • What is HTML?
  • Setup (Editor, Browser).
  • Doc Structure (<html>, <head>, <body>).
  • Headings, Paragraphs, Comments.

Week 2: Text Formatting & Links

Styling text and creating hyperlinks.

  • Bold, Italic, Underline, etc.
  • Phrase Tags (<strong>, <em>).
  • Hyperlinks (<a> tag).
  • Absolute vs Relative Paths.
Go to Week 2

Week 3: Lists & Images

Organizing content and embedding media.

  • Unordered & Ordered Lists.
  • Definition Lists.
  • Images (<img>, src, alt).
  • Image Formats.
Go to Week 3

Week 4: Tables

Structuring data in rows and columns.

  • Table Structure (<table>, <tr>, <th>, <td>).
  • Headers, Body, Footer (<thead>, <tbody>, <tfoot>).
  • Spanning Cells (colspan, rowspan).
  • Captions.
Go to Week 4

Week 5: Forms Part 1

Creating interactive forms.

  • Form Element (<form>).
  • Text, Password, Submit Inputs.
  • Labels (<label>).
  • Input Attributes (name, id, value).
Go to Week 5

Week 6: Forms Part 2

More form controls.

  • Checkboxes & Radio Buttons.
  • Dropdowns (<select>, <option>).
  • Text Areas (<textarea>).
  • Fieldsets & Legends.
Go to Week 6

Week 7: Semantic HTML & Multimedia

Meaningful structure and media.

  • Semantic Elements (<header>, <nav>, <main>, etc.).
  • Audio (<audio>).
  • Video (<video>).
  • Iframes (<iframe>).
Go to Week 7

Week 8: Review & Next Steps

Consolidation and future learning.

  • Review Key Concepts.
  • Best Practices (Validation, Accessibility).
  • HTML Integration with CSS/JS.
  • Next Steps: Intro to CSS.
